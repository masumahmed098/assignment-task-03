Health

Moderate severity

"I can't stop feeling anxious and overwhelmed every day"

⚠ Possible Causes

  • Chronic stress from work or studies
  • Poor sleep patterns and fatigue
  • Overthinking and rumination cycles
  • Lack of physical activity or sunlight

✦ Simple Solutions

  • Practice 5-minute daily mindfulness or deep breathing
  • Limit social media consumption, especially at night
  • Establish a consistent sleep schedule (7-9 hours)
  • Exercise for 20-30 mins, even light walks help

🔗 Recommended Help

BetterHelp Therapy Calm App (Meditation) Talk to a GP
Tech Low severity

"My WiFi keeps disconnecting every few minutes"

⚠ Possible Causes

  • Router firmware is outdated or overheated
  • Too many devices on one network
  • Signal interference from nearby networks
  • Faulty network adapter drivers on device

✦ Simple Solutions

  • Restart router and modem, wait 60 seconds
  • Update router firmware via admin panel
  • Change WiFi channel to less crowded one (use 5Ghz)
  • Update or reinstall network adapter drivers

🔗 Recommended Help

ISP Support Line Reddit r/techsupport Router Admin Guide
